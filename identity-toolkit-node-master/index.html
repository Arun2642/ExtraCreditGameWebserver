Learn how to play:

The Extra Credit Game is a version of the classic itterated prisoner's dilemma simulation. You are a student seeking extra credit on a project you completed with a partner. You can either praise or rat on your partner, as can they, payoffs for each combination of actions are given on the Enter page. Participants must write a javascript program that, given the current round number, and the history of both your, and your oppenents past moves, will return either True representing praising, or False representing ratting.
